// about pixelvault

Built for boring afternoons.

PixelVault is a thin, fast wrapper around the Lumin game network. We don't host the games ourselves — we just give you a clean, fast, dark-mode home to play them in. No ads in your face, no paywalls, no "create an account to continue."

Zero friction

Click a game, it loads. That's the whole experience. No installs, no plugins, no logins.

Always growing

The catalog is pulled live, so when new games drop on the network they show up here automatically.

Made to be shared

Bookmark it, share it with a friend, pin it in your browser — PixelVault works the same on any device.
